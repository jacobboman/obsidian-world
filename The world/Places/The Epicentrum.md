A place where time has been quite broken. It's reminiscent of a black hole. The closer to the center one gets the stronger the effects get. In the close vicinity an observer would see a person approaching the centre from far out at a run, slowing down without stopping to walk.

- There are guardians of said crystal in the perimeter and the world.
