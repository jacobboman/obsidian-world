> *"Spring comes once a year, The Wandering Glenn is not as forseeable."

A mobile ecosystem attached to a person.

A reversed natural disaster.

The embodiment of the Wandering Glenn can be both malign or benign according to the will of the current wielder.

In general though, the effects are neutral.

**Heralds of the Green**
Scouts and messengers that travel along in the evergreen. Whenever they get close to society, a missive is sent out to warn or inform of the arival of The Wandering Glenn
